Wisdom from Mom

Tips & Tricks

Things she learned the hard way so you don't have to.

🫙

Feed your starter consistently

A happy starter gets fed at the same time every day. Think of it like a little pet — it rewards routine.

🌡️

Temperature matters more than time

A warm kitchen means a faster rise. A cool one means slower — but often more flavor. Learn to read your dough, not just the clock.

🤲

Trust the stretch-and-fold

You don't need to knead sourdough like bread dough. Gentle folds every 30 minutes build all the strength you need.

⏳

Cold proof overnight

Pop the shaped dough in the fridge before bed. A cold, slow proof develops richer flavor and makes scoring much easier.

🔪

Score confidently and quickly

A hesitant score drags the dough. One swift, decisive cut at an angle gives you that beautiful ear every time.

🎉

Every failed loaf is a lesson

Dense crumb? Under-proofed. Flat loaf? Weak starter. Mom keeps a little notebook — and now so should you.
